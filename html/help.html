Frequently Asked Questions

What are the library hours?

The library is open Monday through Friday from 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM and on weekends from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

How can I contact a librarian?

You can reach out to a librarian via the "Contact a Librarian" form on our website.

Can I borrow books online?

Yes, registered users can borrow digital copies of select books through our online catalog.

Do I need a membership to access the library?

While membership is required to borrow books, anyone can sign up and read within the library premises.

What is the late fee for overdue books?

There is no late fee since this is a digital library
